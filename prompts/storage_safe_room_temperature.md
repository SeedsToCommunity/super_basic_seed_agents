## Prompt Guidance

**Purpose:** Short-term dry storage at home (weeks to one season).

- Emphasize dry, labeled, pest-safe.
- Do not mention stratification or cold-moist methods.
- Do not recommend desiccants or moisture absorbers.
- Focus on breathable containers (paper envelopes, paper bags) and dry storage locations.
- If this species cannot tolerate drying, state that dry room-temperature storage is not appropriate and suggest alternatives (immediate sowing, moist holding).
