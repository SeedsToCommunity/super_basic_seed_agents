## Prompt Guidance

**Purpose:** Describe container-based drying after collection.

- Include containers that will maintain seed viability.
- Keep seeds/fluff contained.
- Do not describe processing steps.
- Do not suggest exposed surface drying (screens, trays, tarps).
- Do not recommend desiccants, silica gel, or any moisture-absorbing additives.
- If this species tolerates drying, describe air-drying in breathable containers (paper bags, cardboard boxes).
- If this species cannot tolerate drying (recalcitrant seeds), state this clearly and recommend appropriate moist holding or immediate-sow alternatives.
- If extended drying time is needed for proper conditioning, state this explicitly.

## Format & Caps

- Short text
- Max 2 sentences
