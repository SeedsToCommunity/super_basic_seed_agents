## Prompt Guidance

**Purpose:** Document hazards and nuisances during collection.

- Include toxicity, spines, irritants, allergens, sap, fluff, etc.
- Keep factual, non-alarmist tone.
- Separate hazards vs nuisances when both exist.

## Format & Caps

- Paragraph
- Max 5 sentences
